# Operations

HealthData.ai is operated for long-term use in clinical and research-oriented environments. The focus is on stable, traceable, and scalable platform operations.

!!! info "Operating Model"
	In addition to cloud operation, on-premises deployment at the customer site is also possible.

## Monitoring and Operations

Platform operations are continuously monitored.

Capabilities:

- real-time monitoring of central services and interfaces
- automated alerts for critical events
- structured incident and escalation processes

## Backups and Disaster Recovery

A comprehensive backup and recovery concept ensures data availability.

Measures:

- regular backups
- Write-Ahead Logging (WAL)
- offsite storage
- air-gapped backup strategies
- regular restore tests

## Updates and Releases

HealthData.ai is continuously developed and delivered in structured release cycles.

- regular release cycles
- planned maintenance windows
- continuous platform improvement

## Onboarding and Training

The introduction of HealthData.ai follows a structured and guided approach.

- train-the-trainer approaches
- targeted training for different user groups
- implementation support for organizations and networks

## Documentation

- integrated end-user documentation within the platform
- technical documentation for developers and administrators
- continuous content updates
